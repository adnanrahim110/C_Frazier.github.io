About the Author

I am C. Frazier and I am a new author. I started by writing for fun. Then I said why not see what other people think of the book. I am an author with some learning challenges, so that means that at least for me it can be tough to write. That said, that has not nor will it stop me from writing at all. I always turned to books to escape from the world around me when I was younger.

About Book

In the midst of their adventures, Wren, Scáthach, Cipher, and Grey forge deep bonds of friendship and loyalty, relying on each other’s strengths to overcome the trials they face. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the Aler Kingdom, they uncover ancient secrets and hidden truths that test their resolve and push them to their limits. The group must confront their fears and doubts, learning to trust in themselves and each other as they strive to fulfill their destinies and protect the kingdom from dark forces that threaten its very existence. Through courage, determination, and the power of friendship, Wren and her companions embark on a journey that will change their lives forever.

What keeps you Turn the pages?

The author C. Frazier’s writing is engaging and full of action, keeping the reader intrigued and eager to know what happens next. The characters are well-developed and face various challenges that make them relatable and human. The dynamic between the group members adds depth to the story as they navigate obstacles and work together to achieve their goals. The inclusion of mythical creatures and gods adds an element of mystery and fantasy to the narrative, keeping the reader on their toes. Overall, the story is well-paced and keeps the reader turning the pages to see how the characters will overcome their challenges and what new adventures await them.
